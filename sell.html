The Workflow

From Surplus to Sold in Three Steps

We call this the Cyclic flow — standardise, discover, transact.

01

List & Standardise

Log your surplus: fabric type, GSM, width, available quantity, location, certifications, and availability window. CircuLink standardises the data so buyers can compare instantly.

02

Receive & Negotiate

Verified buyers discover your listing and send inquiries. Chat, share samples, and negotiate pricing all within CircuLink. No cold emails, no spreadsheets.

03

Transact & Prove Impact

Payment held in escrow until delivery confirmed. Once complete, both parties receive impact certificates. Over time, your transaction history builds buyer trust.

The key value is not just listings — it's standardised data, structured discovery, and trust that builds with every deal.
